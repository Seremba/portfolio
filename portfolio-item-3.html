Budget App

An app to cater for your budgetting needs

The budget app is used to keep track of your income. It built using HTML,CSS and vanilla javascript. It has two parts; that is income and expenses. You can delete an entry in case it is not what you wanted. The app has the description field where you enter your items such as salary, taxes among others. The app displays the date too
